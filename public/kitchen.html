Know your kitchen layout

Understanding a kitchen layout is the first step to planning a perfect kitchen. Your kitchen would have one of the following layouts.

Parallel

Cabinets placed on two opposite walls for a balanced and efficient layout.

L-shaped Kitchen

2 adjoining countertops with corner spaces.

U-shaped Kitchen

3 connected walls of cabinets with an open entrance.

Core materials we use

Your kitchen goes through a lot. Superior quality materials at the core ensure it lasts a lifetime.

HDF-HMR

Lightweight, budget-friendly with a varying range of load and screw-holding capacities.

Plywood - BWR, BWP

High strength, termite-resistant and low on emission.
